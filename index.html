Mission

Design Hub is about students building solutions to real-world problems in and around the University of Pittsburgh. We are driven by design principles and strive for interdisciplinary collaboration and hands-on experiences. Out vision is to serve as a student ideation platform for generating top-notch ideas and creating cross-disciplinary interactions.
